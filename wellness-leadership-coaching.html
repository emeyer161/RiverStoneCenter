Wellness/Leadership Coaching
“We are what we pretend to be, so we must be careful about what we pretend to be”
Kurt Vonnegut, Mother Night

Wellness coaching explores how you are living and helps you take a look at what makes you happy and well. We look at areas such as health, relationships, family, career, and finances. I focus on your inner conversations that may involve chronic self-criticism and self-doubt, helping you explore feelings, behavior patterns, and self-beliefs that keep you stuck or not moving toward a life of thriving. This process is about standing on your own two feet and moving towards a more fulfilled life. I draw on methods such as self-awareness and Core Energetics which is a combination of talking, emotional release techniques and movement.
