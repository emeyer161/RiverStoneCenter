About Jim Haessig

Owner of Riverstone Center for Massage and Well-Being, Jim is a licensed massage therapist, a certified Wellness Coach from the Mayo Clinic, and a Transformative Touch Somatic Therapist. He graduated from Villanova University with a BS in Science, and completed a 4-year Core Energetics Practitioner Certification program. Jim started his career in the commercial printing industry, owning and managing Plains Printers and then MMP Printing Group for 30 years. A lifetime resident of Morris County, Jim is married to Beth and has three sons, Danny, Peter, and TJ.
